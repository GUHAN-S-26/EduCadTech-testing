About EduCadTech

Welcome to The EduCadTech Learning

EduCadTech is a leading engineering and software training institute founded in 2013 in Chennai, India.

EduCadTech History

EduCadTech was established in 2013 in Chennai, India, with a mission to empower engineering students through practical learning. Over the years, it has expanded across India, becoming a trusted name in CAD/CAM and software training, along with project and research support.

EduCadTech Mission

The vision of EduCadTech is to inspire students to imagine, design, and create a better world through innovation and creativity. Its mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world applications by providing hands-on training, real-time projects, and support in research and development.

EduCadTech Administration

EduCadTech is managed by a team of experienced professionals and skilled faculty with over 20 years of expertise. The institute offers structured training, guidance for final-year projects, and continuous support to ensure students gain industry-ready skills and succeed in their careers.
