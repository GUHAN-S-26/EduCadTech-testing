Frequently Asked Questions

What courses does EduCAD Tech offer?
EduCAD Tech offers courses in CAD/CAM software, including programs for Civil, Mechanical, EEE, ECE, CSE, and IT students.
Who can join EduCAD Tech?
Engineering students, diploma holders, graduates, and even final-year students can join to improve their practical and technical skills.
